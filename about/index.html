:: Who am I?#

Hello there, I’m Fahmi, a Cyber Security enthusiast who is currently focusing on Penetration Testing. Through this blog, I wanted to share my views, interests while preserving my experiences/journey on IT related fields.

I mostly post about CTF write-ups, particularly the boot2root type CTFs, because I believe it improves my methodology/approach in Penetration Testing.

Well, so, all the thoughts and concepts written in this blog are based on my experiences, and that’s why I’ll put some reminders to those who reads my blog:

  • First, don’t trust everything you read on the internet, including what you read on this blog.

  • Second, either you trust it or not, please validate it again.

  • Third, if you find there was a mistake on my blog post(s), please poke me! I provide my contacts below.
